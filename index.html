How It Works

Three Steps to Your Goal

Start tracking in minutes, not hours. No complicated setup, no prior nutrition knowledge needed.

1

Create Your Profile

Enter your height, weight, age and goal. Kilo calculates your personalised daily calorie target using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula — the gold standard in nutrition science.

2

Log Your Meals

Snap a photo, scan a barcode, or search our Saudi food database. Kabsa for Thursday lunch? Shawarma on the way home? Log it in under 30 seconds.

3

Hit Your Goal

Watch your daily ring close as you log. Spot patterns, understand your habits, and make small swaps that compound into real, lasting results.
